Quick Start

Step 1: Import the visual into your Power BI report from AppSource.

Step 2: Drag a numeric measure into the Values field well. Optionally add a dimension to Detail (rows) for row-level data.

Step 3: Use the Format pane to configure bins, colors, and statistics overlays.

Free vs Pro

The Free tier renders a 10-bin histogram with mean and median lines. The Pro tier (via AppSource subscription) unlocks configurable bins (2–100), outlier trimming, normal distribution curve, full statistics panel, and value labels.

Common Questions

Q: The visual shows "Add a numeric field to get started"
A: You need to drag a numeric measure or column into the Values field well on the left panel.

Q: The bins setting doesn't change anything
A: Bin count is locked at 10 in the Free tier. Upgrade to Pro to unlock custom bin counts.

Q: The normal curve option is greyed out
A: The normal distribution curve is a Pro feature. Activate your Pro license via AppSource.

Q: How do I get a Pro license?
A: Search for "Histogram Pro" in Microsoft AppSource and subscribe to the Pro plan.
